Features of the Skyline
This August buy one year and enjoy two years of unlimited Gondola rides. Online only. Ends Sunday 31st August.
Luge

Fast paced freedom. Multiple tracks, stunning views, and fun for all ages. Race your friends or enjoy a scenic ride—Rotorua's Luge is a must-do adventure for everyone.

Gondala

Ride to the top in our eight-seater cabins and enjoy spectacular views of Lake Rotorua. The Gondala is perfect for families, photographers, and anyone wanting a relaxing journey.

Skyswing

Rotorua's highest and fastest adrenaline rush with incredible views. Launch high above the ground for a thrilling experience and a heart-pounding challenge.

Mountain Bike Park

All season Gondala-assisted bike park with trails for every skill level. Enjoy scenic routes, great facilities, and legendary challenges for mountain biking enthusiasts.
